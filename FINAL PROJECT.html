ABOUT HIM

Yeat is a fairly new, now overtaking artist. With a moody style dripping with conciete and mysterious, enigmatic qualities, he is a rising star. His album Afterlyfe hit #2 on Hot 200 Bilboard, with 2093 falling on #1 soon after. His newest project, Lyfestyle, expresses his music to the best of his ability - with A Dangerouse Lyfe, which is going to come soon after, as fans wait with anticipation and excitement for the latest album.

Basic info:

Real name: Noah Smith

Stage name: Yeat, somtimes stylised as YEAT/YEET

Birthday: 26th February, 2000

Star sign: Pisces

Favourite food: Cereal and chips

Name of his label: Lyfestyle Corporations, previously Twizzy Rich
